HOW TO ADD YOUR ACTUAL LOGO
===========================

Your uploaded Morabi Merge Solutions logo (the PNG with chevrons and code dashes)
needs to be placed in the /public folder.

Steps:
1. Save your logo PNG file as:
   C:\Users\Rabail Butt\.gemini\antigravity\scratch\morabi-merge-solutions\public\logo.png

2. Then in Navbar.tsx (line ~84) and Footer.tsx (line ~84), replace:
   <Logo size={32} />
   with:
   <Image src="/logo.png" alt="Morabi Merge Solutions Logo" width={36} height={36} style={{ objectFit: "contain" }} />
   (And restore the import: import Image from "next/image";)

3. In layout.tsx, update the favicon:
   <link rel="icon" href="/logo.png" type="image/png" />

Currently, the site uses a custom SVG Logo component (src/components/Logo.tsx)
that closely matches your brand style, so everything looks great right now too.
